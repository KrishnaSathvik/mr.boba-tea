üìã Key Design Decisions

  • Classic tapioca boba is INCLUDED in every drink ‚Äî not an add-on. This is the base.
  • Only 2 categories: Fruit Tea and Milk Tea. No coffee, no brewed tea, no complexity.
  • Sugar is binary: Sugar or No Sugar. Keep it simple (no 25%/50%/75% levels for now).
  • Ice has 3 levels: Regular, Light, and No Ice. Covers all preferences.
  • 5 extra toppings: Lychee Jelly, Mango Jelly, Coconut Jelly, Strawberry Jelly, Popping Pearls.
  • Flavors TBD: Will be decided after supplier sourcing and local taste testing.
  • No food items. Boba only. This keeps operations clean and focused.
