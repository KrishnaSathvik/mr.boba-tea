Our Story

From Hyderabad cravings to
Khammam's first boba

It started with a simple frustration. Every trip back home to Khammam from the city meant leaving behind the bubble tea shops we'd grown to love. Hyderabad already had 50+ boba shops. Khammam had zero.

We saw the same story everywhere — Gen Z and millennials in Tier 2 cities craving the same experiences their friends in metros posted about on Instagram. Khammam had Domino's and KFC, but nothing for the bubble tea generation. The demand was inevitable — we just decided to be first.

We spent months researching suppliers, testing recipes, and perfecting our formulas. We chose real full cream milk over the powdered creamers most chains use — because once you taste the difference, there's no going back. We sourced premium powders from Tea Planet in Hyderabad, specialty flavors from QQS in Delhi, and authentic matcha from Zawaa.

Then came our secret weapon: Boba Lassi — a fusion of India's beloved lassi with the fun, chewy toppings of bubble tea. It's the gateway drink for anyone who's never tried boba before. Familiar format, surprising twist.

Mr. Boba Tea isn't just a shop — it's Khammam's first taste of a global phenomenon, made with local love and real ingredients. Welcome to the boba revolution.

How We Make It

Fresh. Simple. Delicious. Every cup in under 3 minutes.

Brew

Fresh batch of Assam black or Jasmine green tea every morning

Flavor

Add premium powder for milk tea, syrup for fruit tea, or pulp for lassi

Shake

Shaken with ice for bubble tea, blended for lassi — always frothy

Top

Tapioca pearls, popping boba, or jelly — your choice of toppings

Seal

Cup sealed with our signature film, fat straw, ready to enjoy!
